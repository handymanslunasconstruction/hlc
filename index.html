About Us

Handyman Luna's Construction is a family owned business, we get the task completed and to customer’s satisfaction! We take pride in our work and the results speak for itself. Anywhere from painting to plumbing to electrical, no matter how complicated it seems we can handle it. Our expertise lies in:

  • Electrical.
  • Plumbing.
  • Painting (interior and exterior).
  • Drywall repairs.
  • Backsplash.
  • Demo and repair.
  • Tub installation.
  • Bathroom tile work.
  • Water heater installation.
  • Carpentry.

We do it all! Our hours are between Monday-Saturday from 8am-8pm. Handyman Lunas Construction, we’ll take care of you like family.
